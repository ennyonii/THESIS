Dietary and reproductive information  on Hemigrammus marginatus collected along ecotone areas in Caatinga and Atlantic forest

This dataset was used as part of a Master's thesis in biological research, focusing on the trophic ecology of Hemigrammus marginatus. It was originally developed to test the influence of two distinct biomes from the Brazilian northeast (Caatinga and Atlantic Forest)  on  the reproductive strategy of Hemigrammus marginatus, 
The data (Full_dataset 1 and Full_dataset_2 have been restructured from the original wide-format spreadsheets into a normalized relational database to facilitate advanced ecological analysis, such as dietary shifts across biomes and rainfall gradients.

The database is composed of five interconnected tables, allowing for efficient querying of environmental, specimen-level, and dietary data.

1. sites.csv
Contains the geographic and ecological context for sampling.

2. sampling.csv
Contains information on the sampling events.

3. fish info.csv
Contains data on every individual fish collected.

4. diet info.csv
Contains all identified food items (prey items).

5. relationship.csv
Contains non-zero dietary entry to match individual fish.
